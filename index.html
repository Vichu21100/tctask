About Technical Council

The Technical Council, NIT Tiruchirappalli is a wing of Students' Council, responsible for managing, ideating and supervising most of the activities in the college. The duties and responsibilities of the body mainly revolve around three entities:

  1. Campus : The Technical Council works for campus development, spearheading initiatives, organising TechTalks, workshops, a prominent example being the DSC workshops and contacting technical companies for giveaways for the students. Moreover, the Technical Council also coordinates with the CoEs, ISRO incubation centre and other labs to ensure a thriving platform for budding tech enthusiasts.
  2. Pragyan: The Technical Council ideates upon and implements, 'Technical Showcases' (videos and setups utilising a prominent technology to showcase the number of days to go for Pragyan), organises Gamescape, and conducts initiatives like the Human Library. The Technical Council also aids in the conduction of events during Pragyan.
  3. Techno-Managerial Clubs: The Technical Council, along with the Technical Secretary, monitors and manages the activities of the various techno-managerial clubs on campus.

Crypto NITT

  • Official blockchain community of NITT
  • Organized a hands-on web3 workshop. This workshop focused on immediately getting started building on the blockchain.

Google Developer Student Clubs

  • This community focusses on developing technologies that help to connect ,collaborate and grow in a peer-to-peer learning environment.
  • Collaborated with TC, Delta Force and Spider to organize Android Study Jams 2022.This gave the opportunity for many to kick start their Android Development journey.

TecOS

  • Official open source community of NITT
  • An “How-to” series was launched on the YouTube channel of TC.Here, Satyamurti D from the Delta Force shared his knowledge on “How to GIT”.

Women Inclusivity Network NITT

  • Formed by women students and alumni of NITT.
  • Focusses on the representation of women in STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering and mathematics) fields.
  • Recently conducted a workshop #IamRemarkable to celebrate the power of diversity.

About Pragyan

  • Pragyan, NIT Trichy is the technical festival of the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli.
  • It is one of the largest techno-managerial fests in India.
  • It is also the first student-run organisation in the world to get an ISO 20121:2012 certification for Sustainable Event Management.
